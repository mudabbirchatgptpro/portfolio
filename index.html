Selected work

Adaptive Tutoring Engine

Independent build · learning science + full-stack

Problem: most "adaptive" practice just reshuffles a fixed bank; it does not know when a student has actually mastered a skill or run out of questions.

Built: an engine with a Bayesian Knowledge Tracing mastery model, interleaved retrieval practice across a skill knowledge graph, and agentic item generation that writes and verifies new questions the moment the bank for a skill is exhausted.

Result: a working system that keeps each learner at the edge of their ability and never runs dry. The mechanic above is a self-contained slice of it.

QuriOS

Consumer EdTech product for children · qurios.io

Problem: kids' screen time is a fight between engagement and learning, and parents have no dial for it.

Built: an adaptive learning system on Electron and Supabase with Claude-powered per-student voice and hints, and a parent console that sets the balance between play and structured practice, deciding where AI accelerates and where a human stays in the loop.

Result: a shipped product with per-student adaptation and parent controls, the same AI-versus-human-connection boundary a learner platform has to get right.

Kid Reels

Attention-design prototype · live demo

Problem: a short-video feed optimizes for time-on-app, not for what a parent wants a child to actually get out of it.

Built: a parent-controlled reels feed that plays real video but injects a "three fun, then one learning" pattern the parent sets, changing the incentive of the feed itself.

Result: a live, keyless demo showing engagement and learning designed together rather than traded off.

Agentic AI workflows

How I work, not just what I ship

Built: multi-agent workflows that decompose a question, run parallel researchers each blind to the others, adversarially verify every claim before accepting it, and return a cited synthesis, with human-gated steps for anything that leaves the machine.

Result: research and analysis that used to take days takes minutes, and I start from a checked draft instead of a blank page. This is what "AI across the entire workflow" looks like in practice.
